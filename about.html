About

Survey hub is a website designed to make your life easier by providing a way for you to collect data for your surveys without having to hunt for it online. It achieves this by allowing anyone to upload their own survey and providing credits to those who complete other peoples surveys. credits can then be spent in order to boost your own surveys allowing all other users to see them as they appear at the top of survey hubs homepage.
